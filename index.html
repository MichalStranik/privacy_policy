2) Information we collect

We do not require account registration and we do not directly collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising as described below.

3) Third-party services

a) Advertising (AdMob)

The App displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information (such as advertising identifiers), IP address, and ad interaction data to provide, measure, and improve ads, and to help prevent fraud.

b) Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

The App uses Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used (for example, which screens are opened, session duration, and basic device/app information). This helps improve stability and user experience.

c) Crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to diagnose errors and improve reliability. Crash reports may include device information, app version, and information about the circumstances of the crash.

We do not receive your advertising identifier directly, but Google services may process it as part of providing these features.

4) Data stored on your device

The App stores some data locally on your device (such as game progress, preferences, or cache). This data is not sent to us as part of normal use.

5) Permissions

The App requests Internet access to load ads and to send analytics/crash data to Google services. No other sensitive permissions are required.

6) Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7) Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be processed by Google as described above (AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics) to provide advertising, analytics, and crash reporting.
